## App Review Reply (Ready to Paste)

Hello App Review Team,

Thank you for the detailed feedback. We have addressed all reported points for version 1.0:

### Guideline 2.3.8 - Accurate Metadata (App Icon)
- We replaced placeholder assets with finalized production app icons.
- The same brand identity is used consistently across all iOS icon sizes, including the 1024x1024 App Store icon.

### Guideline 1.2 - Safety (User-Generated Content)
- Users must accept Terms of Use before accessing the app.
- Terms explicitly state zero tolerance for objectionable content and abusive behavior.
- In-app protections implemented:
  - content filtering before sending text messages
  - user report flow (report user / report message)
  - user blocking flow
  - report+block option directly from chat messages
- Blocking now creates an immediate moderation signal for our team and blocked users are removed from further message delivery to the blocker.
- We process abuse reports within 24 hours and remove violating content/users.

### Guideline 2.1(b) - Business Model Clarification
Answers to your questions:

1. **Who are the users that will use paid subscriptions in the app?**  
   We currently do not provide paid subscriptions in this app version.

2. **Where can users purchase subscriptions that can be accessed in the app?**  
   There is no subscription purchase flow in the app and no external subscription sales for app features.

3. **What specific types of previously purchased subscriptions can a user access in the app?**  
   None. The app does not unlock any previously purchased subscription content.

4. **What paid content, subscriptions, or features are unlocked within the app that do not use In-App Purchase?**  
   None. No paid digital content or paid feature unlocks are available in the app.
